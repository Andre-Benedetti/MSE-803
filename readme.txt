# MSE-803 W1-A1: Data Analysis and Aggregation Project

## The Data Story
By applying aggregation functions (Sum, Mean, and Count), we transformed raw transactional data into strategic insights:

1. Market Leadership: Category B is the top performer, leading in both total sales revenue (22,154) and quantity of items sold (343).
2. Sales Efficiency:Although Category A has a lower total volume, it maintains a strong average sale value (545.76), indicating a higher price point per unit.
3. Transaction Volume:Category B also recorded the highest frequency of sales (36 records), showing high market demand and consistency.
